About Me

Hi, I'm Lauren — a fourth-year Political Science and Philosophy scholarship student at McGill University, currently based in Montreal during the school year and Toronto over the summer.

With years of experience tutoring elementary and high school students, I specialize in reading, writing, social sciences, and offer general academic support. I'm a graduate of a French Immersion program and comfortable supporting students in French-language subjects at the elementary level.

Whether your child needs help catching up, staying motivated, or getting ahead, I'm here to support them every step of the way.
